## ğŸ¯ Quick Start by Role

### For Managers
1. **Login** with your email and password
2. **Navigate** to the Crew Management section
3. **Create** crew member accounts
4. **Monitor** their progress through the dashboard
5. **Review** quiz submissions when completed
6. **Generate** certificates upon successful completion

### For Crew Members
1. **Check your email** for the magic link
2. **Click the link** to access the system
3. **Complete** each training phase in order
4. **Upload** photo proof as required
5. **Take the quiz** when all phases are complete
6. **Download** your certificate after approval
